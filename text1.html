Inspirational Background

Written Art

I have always found writing as a safe space. Whether it is for personal use such as journaling or for entertaining others, it always brings me peace. Over the years, I have written fiction, poetry, music, and articles for a local magazine. I hope to use this creativity for screenwriting and novels someday.

Visual Art
